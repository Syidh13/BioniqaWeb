About Us

Bioniqa Global Pte Ltd (PT Bio Teknologi Semesta) is a leading innovator in the field of carbon capture and sustainability. Founded in 2023, our company is dedicated to harnessing the power of algae to create cutting-edge technologies that can help businesses and individuals reduce their carbon footprint and contribute to a greener future.

Our passionate and experienced team of experts is committed to pushing the boundaries of what is possible in carbon capture technology. By continually investing in research and development, we aim to deliver solutions that have a meaningful impact on the environment and the global community.
